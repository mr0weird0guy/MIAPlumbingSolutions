Covid-19

To Our Valued Customers:

At MIA Technical Services our guiding principle has always been to “Take care of our customers and our team members”. This principle rings loud and clear as we take steps to prevent the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19). Coolray Companies are dedicated to the health, wellness and safety of our customers and employees, and we are monitoring the dynamic COVID-19 situation to ensure we are taking all necessary proactive measures.

MIA Companies remain committed and prepared to respond to our customers’ needs immediately and to provide the same level of trusted and quality service you have come to expect.

To that end, we are following the guidelines and safety tips outlined by the Center for Disease Control (CDC) and the World Health Organization (WHO). In addition, we are requiring employees to stay home and seek medical attention if they are experiencing symptoms related to COVID-19 or have been exposed to COVID-19.

We understand that the services we provide are essential to everyday life and your comfort at home. If you need us, please do not hesitate to call us at 770-421-8400 to schedule service. Our office staff and technicians are here to help you as needed with any of your heating, cooling, plumbing or electrical needs.

We care deeply about the well-being of our customers and our team members. We will continue to provide our services within the guidelines of the CDC and WHO and will continue to communicate necessary information to you as it is available.

Thank you for trusting MIA Technical Services to serve you.

Sincerely,

Andy Piercefield
President
